Qianlong Emperor (1711–1799)

Qianlong was one of China's longest-reigning emperors. He expanded the empire and left a cultural legacy.

Timeline

1735 - Ascended to the throne.

1755-1760 - Expanded Qing territory.

1796 - Abdicated after 60 years.

Legacy

Qianlong’s rule is remembered for its military success and artistic achievements.
